How it works

From 100 feeds to one signal

Weft runs a continuous pipeline to turn raw RSS volume into structured, scored, searchable news.

1

Fetch from 100+ RSS feeds

Weft continuously polls curated RSS feeds across tech, AI, business, science, world news, politics, sports, and culture. No paywalls, no algorithms — just sources you'd actually read.

2

Score with AI

Each article is scored 1–10 by AI based on your interest profile. Articles covering the same story from multiple sources get cross-referenced and flagged as trending.

3

Generate summaries on demand

When you open an article, Weft generates an AI summary tailored to your preferred style — concise, key points, or simple explanation — using Groq's fast inference API.

4

Deliver the daily briefing

Each day, a full AI briefing synthesizes the day's themes, top stories, and emerging narratives across all categories — your morning intelligence briefing.

Sign in to set your interest profile, save articles, and get a daily briefing tuned to you. Free, always.
